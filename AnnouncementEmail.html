Season 2

 

It's that time of the year again!

With Season 2, we've rewritten the app from the ground up and introduced some new features. 


For previous players and newcomers -

 

The gist of the game is to follow the NFL season and every week choose a team that will win. If your team wins, you advance to the next week. If your team loses, you're out of the game. What makes this challenging is that you can only pick the same NFL team once the whole season. This continues until there is only one player left standing who takes the entire pot.

 

This year we've added multi-team support. Entry is 10$ per team you create.
