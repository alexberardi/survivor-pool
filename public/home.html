The first game has begun! Good luck everyone!

We are still in the process of collecting everyone's entry but as soon as we have the finalized pot amount, we'll display it on the site.

We'd also like to take a moment and discuss transparency. As a rule, the picks of the site admins (Jimmy & Alex) will be posted every week on the site (eventually on the Make a Pick page) and the picks will be made at the earliest day possible of the week. This is an effort to keep this game as honest as possible while also retaining the spirit of competition.

So for the first week, Jimmy has chosen the Texans and Alex has chosen the Seahawks.

In other news, we have plenty of features to work on throughout the season for a 1.1 version of surivor pool. Expect some more fun features to come. But if you have any suggestions, please don't hesitate to talk to us or shoot us an email. We don't bite.
